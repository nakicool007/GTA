Notable Players

  • Jatin Dhaiya



    - Professional tennis player from India.
    - Career–high ITF singles ranking: No. 730.
    - Career–high ATP singles ranking: No. 1077 (28 Oct 2019).
    - Career–high doubles ranking: No. 1360.
    - AITA National Ranking: No. 30 (March 2021).
    - Active on ITF World Tennis Tour with 200+ matches across singles & doubles.

  • Rishika Sunkara



    - Indian professional player with multiple ITF titles.
    - Highest WTA singles ranking: No. 441 (9 Nov 2015).
    - Highest WTA doubles ranking: No. 375 (5 Aug 2013).
    - Highest ITF singles ranking: No. 76 (per CoreTennis).
    - AITA National Ranking: reached No. 25 in Women’s Singles (Apr 2021).
    - Represented India in national and international tournaments.

  • Nakul Kumbria



    - NCAA Division II athlete representing the University of Texas–Permian Basin (UTPB) Falcons.
    - Played both singles and doubles from 2019–2022.
    - Notable doubles win with partner Conner Everett in 2021–22 season.
    - Known for versatility across formats and contributions to team competitions.
    - Former Indian junior player, highly ranked in AITA U-16 and U-18 categories.
